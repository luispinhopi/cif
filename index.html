About.

Collectif is a community about photography, it has a
photography book shop, it’s an academy with
courses and workshops about photography, and it’s
also a gallery. It’s a space that lives and breathes photography.
It works in a unique way that sets it apart from many others that might look similar.
Collectif brings people and art together, educating them and helping to broaden horizons.
